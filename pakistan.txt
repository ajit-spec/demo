Pakistan is a country in South Asia known for its diverse cultures, languages, and landscapes.
It is the world's fifth-most populous country with a population exceeding 225 million people.
It has a rich history dating back to ancient civilizations such as the Indus Valley.
Pakistan has a varied climate, ranging from arid deserts to lush green valleys.
Pakistan is bordered by India to the east, Afghanistan and Iran to the west, and China to the north.
Pakistan's official languages are Urdu and English.
